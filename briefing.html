Subject:

48-Hour Site Load Audit Requirements

Objective

To capture high-resolution, real-time power consumption data ensuring the Solar Mini-Grid is precisely engineered for current peak demand and projected 5-year growth.

1. Strategic Criticality

  • A CAPEX Optimization: Eliminates capital waste by preventing "over-sizing." Precision data ensures the client only pays for necessary capacity.
  • B Operational Uptime: Correctly sizes the battery bank to handle nighttime peaks, preventing system blackouts during critical hours.
  • C Financial Bankability: Provides the data required by institutional lenders (DFIs) to validate ROI and fuel displacement figures.

2. Audit Methodology

Phase I: Deployment

Installation of industrial-grade data loggers at the Main Distribution Board. This is a non-invasive process requiring zero downtime.

Phase II: Logging

Continuous monitoring for 48-72 hours, capturing voltage, current, and harmonics at 5-minute intervals.

3. Requirements from Client

Physical Access Mandatory access to Power House & Panels.
Operational State Normal facility operations (Full Load).
Technical Liaison Designated site electrician for assistance.
